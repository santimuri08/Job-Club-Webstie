# Problem Statement

## The Problem

NJIT students interested in AI careers face three major challenges:

### 1. Lack of Structured Guidance
- Don't know which AI career path fits them
- Overwhelmed by learning options
- No clear roadmap to follow

### 2. Incomplete Professional Presence
- LinkedIn profiles are empty or outdated
- GitHub has no meaningful projects
- No portfolio website
- No way for recruiters to find them

### 3. Isolation
- Learning AI alone is hard
- No community of peers
- Limited access to mentors
- Miss opportunities they don't know about

---

## The Impact

Without addressing these challenges:
- Students miss internship/job opportunities
- Graduate unprepared for AI job market
- Lose motivation without community support
- Fall behind peers at other schools

---

## Our Solution: Job Club

### Automated Onboarding
- Personalized checklist based on career goals
- Identifies gaps (missing LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.)
- Sends actionable tasks via email

### Community Connection
- Discord server for peer support
- Regular events and workshops
- Mentorship opportunities

### Resource Library
- Guides for building professional presence
- Templates for portfolios
- Career path roadmaps

### Progress Tracking
- CRM tracks each student's journey
- Automated reminders
- No one falls through the cracks

---
