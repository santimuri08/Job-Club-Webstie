# User Personas

## Persona 1: AI-Curious Freshman - "Alex"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 18
- **Year:** Freshman
- **Major:** Computer Science
- **Location:** On-campus, Newark NJ

### Background
Alex just started at NJIT and has heard about AI but doesn't know where to begin. They've done some Python in high school but have no professional experience.

### Goals
- Understand AI career paths
- Build first AI projects
- Meet other students interested in AI
- Start building LinkedIn presence

### Pain Points
- Overwhelmed by options (ML, data science, AI engineering?)
- Doesn't know what skills to learn first
- No professional network
- Empty LinkedIn/GitHub profiles

### Needs from Job Club
- Clear beginner-friendly guidance
- Step-by-step checklist
- Community of peers
- Mentorship opportunities

### Quote
> "I know AI is important but I don't know where to start."
