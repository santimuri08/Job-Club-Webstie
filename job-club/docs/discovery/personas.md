# User Personas

## Persona 1: AI-Curious Freshman - "Alex"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 18 |
| **Year** | Freshman |
| **Major** | Computer Science |
| **Location** | On-campus, Newark NJ |
| **Tech Comfort** | Moderate |

### Background
Alex just started at NJIT and has heard about AI but doesn't know where to begin. They've done some Python in high school but have no professional experience. They're excited about the possibilities but feel overwhelmed by all the buzzwords—ML, deep learning, data science, prompt engineering.

### Goals
- Understand AI career paths and which one fits them
- Build first AI projects to add to portfolio
- Meet other students interested in AI
- Start building LinkedIn presence early
- Find internship opportunities for sophomore year

### Pain Points
- Overwhelmed by options (ML, data science, AI engineering?)
- Doesn't know what skills to learn first
- No professional network
- Empty LinkedIn/GitHub profiles
- Imposter syndrome around more experienced students

### Behaviors
- Spends time on YouTube watching AI tutorials
- Follows AI influencers on Twitter/X
- Hesitant to attend events alone
- Prefers structured learning over self-directed

### Needs from Job Club
- Clear beginner-friendly guidance
- Step-by-step checklist with achievable milestones
- Community of peers at similar level
- Mentorship opportunities with upperclassmen
- Beginner-friendly workshops

### Quote
> "I know AI is important but I don't know where to start."

### How Job Club Helps Alex
1. **Onboarding checklist** breaks down overwhelming tasks into steps
2. **Career goal selection** helps narrow focus
3. **Discord community** provides peer support
4. **Resource library** offers beginner guides
5. **Events** give structured learning opportunities

## Persona 2: Career-Switching Senior - "Jordan"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 22 |
| **Year** | Senior |
| **Major** | Information Technology |
| **Location** | Commuter, Jersey City |
| **Tech Comfort** | High |

### Background
Jordan is graduating in 6 months and realized they need AI skills to be competitive in the job market. They have solid programming experience from coursework and a part-time IT job, but no AI-specific portfolio. They've been applying to jobs but getting rejected due to lack of AI experience.

### Goals
- Get AI job before graduation (urgent!)
- Build impressive portfolio FAST
- Network with recruiters and hiring managers
- Stand out from other candidates
- Transition from general IT to AI/ML roles

### Pain Points
- Limited time (graduating in 6 months)
- No AI projects to show employers
- LinkedIn is basic/outdated (last updated freshman year)
- Missed internship opportunities—now playing catch-up
- Resume doesn't highlight AI-relevant skills

### Behaviors
- Applies to 10+ jobs per week
- Watches "how to get hired" content
- Compares self to peers with internships
- Stressed about post-graduation plans
- Willing to put in extra hours to catch up

### Needs from Job Club
- Quick wins for portfolio (projects they can build fast)
- LinkedIn optimization help
- Networking events with employers
- Interview prep resources
- Resume review for AI positions

### Quote
> "I need to build my AI portfolio in the next 3 months or I'm stuck."

### How Job Club Helps Jordan
1. **Personalized checklist** identifies exactly what's missing
2. **LinkedIn guide** helps optimize profile quickly
3. **Portfolio templates** accelerate project showcase
4. **Events** connect them with recruiters
5. **Discord community** provides accountability

## Persona 3: Entrepreneurial Student - "Sam"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 20 |
| **Year** | Junior |
| **Major** | CS + Business Minor |
| **Location** | On-campus, Newark NJ |
| **Tech Comfort** | High |

### Background
Sam wants to start an AI consulting business or startup before graduating. They have strong technical skills and have built several personal projects. They're business-minded and see AI as an opportunity to create value—not just get a job. They've done some freelance work but nothing AI-specific yet.

### Goals
- Build AI consulting portfolio that attracts clients
- Find first paying clients while still in school
- Connect with mentors who've built AI businesses
- Learn the business side of AI (pricing, proposals, contracts)
- Establish professional credibility

### Pain Points
- No professional credibility yet (still a student)
- Don't know how to find clients
- Need mentorship from founders/entrepreneurs
- Portfolio shows projects but not consulting/business skills
- No system for booking client calls

### Behaviors
- Active on LinkedIn, posts about AI topics
- Attends startup events and pitch competitions
- Reads business books alongside technical content
- Networks aggressively
- Already thinking about personal brand

### Needs from Job Club
- Portfolio templates specifically for consultants
- Networking with entrepreneurs and founders
- Mentorship matching with AI business owners
- Calendly/booking setup help
- Guide on pricing AI consulting services

### Quote
> "I want to get paid for AI work before I graduate."

### How Job Club Helps Sam
1. **Calendly setup guide** enables client booking
2. **Consulting portfolio template** showcases business value
3. **Networking events** connect them with potential mentors
4. **Discord community** includes entrepreneurial students
5. **Resource library** covers business aspects of AI

## Design Implications

### For Alex (Beginners)
- Use simple, jargon-free language
- Provide step-by-step instructions
- Show progress indicators
- Highlight community aspects

### For Jordan (Job Seekers)
- Emphasize speed and efficiency
- Show clear outcomes ("Get hired")
- Provide templates and shortcuts
- Feature employer connections

### For Sam (Entrepreneurs)
- Highlight business/consulting resources
- Show credibility-building features
- Emphasize networking opportunities
- Include Calendly/booking setup
